This is a timeline of events in AI and other fields as I experienced them in my life. I started following the developements in singularity adjacent fields in 2015. I also added future predictions of mine. I don't think it makes sense to continue the timeline after 2030, as the world will be so different that it is impossible to predict.

Future
2024 Q2 Open source models are better than GPT-4 (Mistral-Next, Llama3, etc), Image/3D/Sound models all reach the point of being photorealistic. First useful agent systems become available. (GAIA: 30-40%) AI assistants will start to appear with limited capabilities at first

2026 AI starts to automate parts of the economy. This not only includes digital work, but also more and more industries through robots. Most digital content is produced by AI. anti-aging drugs become available, World econemy is mostly dictated by availability of energy and computing power. Quantum computers are used in many industries to solve specific problems (>10000 Qbits)
2030 AI is part of every part of life and is fastly more capable than humans. Aging can be stopped, and reversed in parts. Nearly all illneses can be fixed and we have control over our own DNA. VR/AR is the platform that is used most of the time to view and interact with the internet. BCIs are used by many. We have a base on the moon and start space mining
